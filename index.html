    • Tune That Name

    Create Spotify playlists using the names of people in your iOS contacts.

    Project maintained by mcsearchin Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    Create personalized Spotify playlists containing song titles matching the names of people in your phone contacts. Using the Echo Nest as a search engine, Tune That Name allows you to set the mood with basic song preferences or dig deeper, choosing from lists of songs for each name.

    With a Spotify premium account, you can preview your music and then save it for later listening.

    Suggested uses:

    • Throw a party and create a playlist containing songs dedicated to each of your attendees.
    • Woo your new love interest with a playlist chocked full of songs about him/her.
    • Get over your latest breakup with a cathartic collection about your ex.

    Enjoy! Tune That Name is a fun way to discover new music and create playlists with a personal touch.
